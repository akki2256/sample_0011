Who We Are

Established in 2015, we stand with patients at Tata Memorial Hospital and beyond—and we are growing across India with partners who share our vision.

About Us

Utsaah Foundation is a non-profit organisation registered under the Bombay Public Trusts Act, 1950. Established in 2015, the foundation works extensively in the areas pertaining to treatment of cancer and looks after the well-being of patients, their families and caregivers.

The services rendered by the foundation include:

  • Accommodation support
  • Blood platelet navigation
  • Diagnostic support
  • Financial assistance
  • Emotional and counselling support
  • Support services

Our story

In 2015 Utsaah Foundation was established with the aim to be a source of support and assistance to patients taking treatment at Tata Memorial Hospital & other hospitals, and to identify important resources that would help them to manage their lives through treatment and beyond.

We have commenced our operations at Tata Memorial Hospital, Varanasi and are looking to expand our footprints in different states to serve cancer patients across India. We are seeking out CSR Partnerships to scale up these projects.

Our uniqueness is that we have those fighters who successfully won the battle against cancer and now they are willing to be steadfast companions with other fighters to win their battle.

🎯

Our Mission

  • To create awareness among patients and their families regarding the various resources available to support their battle to overcome cancer.
  • To assist patients to procure maximum financial support to ease the burden, sustain and complete their treatment.
  • To motivate and empower patients and their caregivers to take active responsibility for the treatment. To create an atmosphere of understanding and empathy to remove the fear of cancer, and to instil hope & positivity to overcome their illness.
🌟

Our Vision

A world where every cancer patient and their family has access to practical support, compassionate care, and hope—regardless of where they come from or their financial situation.

🤝

Our Values

Compassion, dignity, integrity, and community. We are driven by the belief that every life matters and that support during illness can transform outcomes and restore hope.
