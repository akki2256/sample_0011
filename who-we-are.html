About Utsaah Foundation

Utsaah Foundation is a non-profit organisation based in Mumbai that works primarily to support cancer patients and their families. We focus on helping patients undergoing treatment at Tata Memorial Hospital by providing financial assistance, guidance, and essential resources needed during treatment.

We help arrange food, accommodation, medicines, blood donors, and other support services for patients who often travel from different parts of India for treatment. In addition, we offer emotional support and counselling, often involving cancer survivors who help guide and encourage patients through their recovery journey.

Our overall aim is to ensure that cancer patients and their families receive both practical and emotional help while dealing with the challenges of treatment.

🎯

Our Mission

To ensure no cancer patient or family faces the journey alone. We provide financial aid, essential resources, and emotional support so that every person undergoing treatment at Tata Memorial Hospital can focus on healing.

🌟

Our Vision

A world where every cancer patient and their family has access to practical support, compassionate care, and hope—regardless of where they come from or their financial situation.

🤝

Our Values

Compassion, dignity, integrity, and community. We are driven by the belief that every life matters and that support during illness can transform outcomes and restore hope.
