Who Do We Work With

Our partners, hospitals, and the communities we serve.

Utsaah Foundation works closely with Tata Memorial Hospital, one of India’s leading cancer care centres. We support patients who are undergoing treatment there by providing financial assistance, accommodation, food, medicines, blood donor linkages, and emotional support so that they can complete their treatment with dignity and hope.

We are grateful to individual donors, corporates, and like-minded NGOs who support our work. Your contributions help us reach more families and provide sustained assistance during their treatment journey.

🏥

Hospitals & Care Centres

We work primarily with Tata Memorial Hospital and support patients referred from there, as well as families in need who travel to Mumbai from across India.

🧑

Doctors & Medical Teams

We collaborate with treating doctors and hospital staff to understand patient needs and channel our support where it matters most.

👥

Survivors & Volunteers

Cancer survivors and volunteers are at the heart of our emotional support and counselling efforts, offering hope and practical guidance to patients and families.
