Accommodation Options

Many families journey far from home to reach cancer centres where treatment is available, and that travel often continues through multiple follow-up appointments. Since patients are not always admitted for the full duration of care, arranging a dependable and budget-friendly stay becomes a major concern for caregivers. To support them during this period, we have put together a practical list of free and low-cost accommodation options from different parts of the country.
