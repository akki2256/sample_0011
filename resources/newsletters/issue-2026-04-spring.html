Spring community round-up

This spring we expanded weekend food support at Tata Memorial Hospital, welcomed new volunteers, and hosted a caregiver listening circle. Families told us that reliable meals and a calm place to talk made the hardest days a little easier.

Volunteer teams now cover two additional shifts near the outpatient block, and we are piloting a simple SMS reminder for families who sign up for navigation appointments so fewer people miss the window for financial counselling.

Thank you to everyone who donated, shared our posts, and showed up in person. The next issue will focus on our accommodation network—watch this space.
