What We Do

Our programmes and initiatives to support cancer patients and their families.

We work primarily with patients undergoing treatment at Tata Memorial Hospital, offering end-to-end support so that families can focus on recovery.

💰

Financial Assistance

Direct financial support for treatment-related costs, travel, and daily needs so that families from across India can afford to stay in Mumbai during treatment.

🍴

Food & Accommodation

We help arrange safe, affordable stay and nutritious food for patients and their attendants who travel long distances for treatment at Tata Memorial Hospital.

💉

Medicines & Blood

Support in arranging essential medicines and connecting patients with blood donors when needed, reducing the burden on families during critical phases of treatment.

❤

Emotional Support & Counselling

Emotional support and counselling for patients and families, often with the involvement of cancer survivors who guide and encourage others through their recovery journey.
