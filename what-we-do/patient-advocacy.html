Patient Advocacy

We believe in the power of advocacy to drive change and are committed to advocating for policies that make a real difference in the lives of cancer patients. Over the next three years, we are committed to collaborating closely with stakeholders to advocate for the inclusion of targeted therapies and molecular tests in the Ayushman Bharat Program.

Additionally, we are actively working to ensure that quarterly CT Scans and PET CT Scans, which are the standard of care for lung cancer patients, are also incorporated into the scheme.

Our advocacy efforts focus on

  • Stigma-Free Understanding of Cancer

    We bring focus to causes of cancer emphasizing the importance of broader awareness and earlier detection strategies for all individuals facing this disease.

  • Palliative Care Connections

    We connect patients nearing the end of life in villages and rural India to local palliative care centers, helping them to die pain-free and with dignity.

  • Notifiable Disease Status

    Through our advocacy efforts, cancer has been listed as a notifiable disease in the state of Jharkhand, India. Additionally, economically vulnerable patients in Jharkhand can now receive ₹10 lakhs for their lung cancer treatment.
